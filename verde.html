Relax & Enjoy the Beauty of Verde Island

Discover the stunning landscapes, rich marine biodiversity, and exciting activities of Verde Island, located in the Verde Island Passage between Luzon and Mindoro, Philippines. This island is renowned for its pristine beaches, unique rock formations, and vibrant marine life, making it a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Unveil the Beauty of Verde Island

Verde Island, situated in the Verde Island Passage, is a small volcanic island known for its stunning beaches and rich marine biodiversity. It is a popular destination for divers and nature enthusiasts.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Mahabang Buhangin: A kilometer-long stretch of white sand beach perfect for swimming and snorkeling.
  • Cueva Sitio: A cave that offers a unique exploration experience.
  • Marine Life: The area is renowned for its diverse marine life, including over 500 species of corals and various sea turtles.
  • Scenic Views: Offers breathtaking views of the surrounding islands and waters.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Snorkeling and Diving: Explore the vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
  • Island Hopping: Visit nearby islands for additional adventures.
  • Hiking: Hike to scenic viewpoints and explore the island's lush landscapes.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of the island and its marine life.

Verde Island: Diving and Dining by the Sea

Verde Island is a top destination for divers, and it offers a number of coastal restaurants where you can enjoy freshly prepared seafood. The dining experience here is typically informal, focusing on local catch-of-the-day dishes like grilled fish, seafood pasta, and sinigang na isda (fish in sour broth). Pair your meal with a cold coconut shake to complete the island experience.

Why Visit Verde Island?

  • Marine Biodiversity: Recognized as the "Center of the Center of Marine Biodiversity" globally.
  • Adventure: Perfect for snorkeling, diving, and island hopping.
  • Accessibility: Located near Batangas City, making it a convenient weekend getaway.
  • Island Access: Open daily; access via boat from Batangas City Port.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Island Access: Open daily; access via boat from Batangas City Port.
  • Entrance Fees: Minimal environmental fees collected by local authorities.
  • Guided Tours: Available for snorkeling and diving activities.

Fun Fact:

Verde Island is part of the Verde Island Passage, which is considered one of the most biodiverse marine areas in the world, hosting over 500 species of corals and numerous marine species
