"Creating Magical Memories"

Enchanted Kingdom (EK) is the Philippines' first and most popular world-class theme park, located in Santa Rosa, Laguna. It officially opened on October 19, 1995, and is known for providing magical experiences through rides, attractions, and entertainment for all ages.
