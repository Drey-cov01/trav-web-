Relax & Rejuvenate at Luljetta's Hanging Gardens and Spa”

Discover the ultimate relaxation experience at Luljetta's Hanging Gardens and Spa, located in Antipolo, Rizal, Philippines. Perched on a mountainside, this serene wellness destination offers stunning views of Laguna de Bay and the Manila skyline, making it a perfect escape for those seeking peace and tranquility.

Unveil the Beauty of Luljetta's Hanging Gardens

Luljetta’s is part of Loreland Farm Resort and is renowned as the first and only hanging gardens spa in the Philippines. Its unique blend of nature, wellness, and luxury makes it a standout destination for relaxation.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Mountain Views: Offers panoramic vistas of Laguna de Bay and the Manila skyline.
  • Wellness Amenities: Includes infinity pools, saunas, jacuzzis, and hydro-massage pools.
  • Serenity Gardens: Features lush hanging gardens that provide a tranquil environment.
  • Traditional Touches: Guests are welcomed with ginger tea and provided with robes for a complete spa experience.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Swimming: Relax in infinity pools with stunning views.
  • Nature Walks: Stroll through the hanging gardens for a refreshing experience.
  • Photography: Capture Instagram-worthy moments with scenic backdrops.
  • Event Hosting: Use the serene spaces for intimate events or celebrations.

Luljetta’s Hanging Gardens and Spa: A Feast for the Senses

Luljetta’s Hanging Gardens and Spa offers a tranquil dining experience amidst nature. The spa has a restaurant that serves fresh, wholesome meals, often featuring local ingredients and Filipino specialties. Guests can enjoy breakfast or light meals while surrounded by lush greenery and calming water features. Their menu typically includes dishes like silog (breakfast meals with eggs, rice, and a variety of meats) and fresh fruit smoothies, perfect for a healthy, relaxing meal.

Why Visit Luljetta's Hanging Gardens?

  • Relaxation Haven: Perfect for unwinding with its spa treatments and serene ambiance.
  • Unique Experience: Combines wellness, nature, and luxury in one destination.
  • Accessibility: Located just an hour away from Metro Manila.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Monday-Friday & Sunday: 10 AM - 9 PM Saturday: 10 AM - 11 PM
  • Day passes start at ₱1,150 per person (inclusive of snacks). Additional fees apply for massages or set meals.

Fun Fact:

Luljetta’s Hanging Gardens is not just a spa but also a wellness sanctuary that promotes eco-friendly practices. It has become a favorite getaway spot for those looking to relax while being close to nature.
