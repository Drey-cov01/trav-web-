Relax & Enjoy the Adventure of Fortune Island

Discover the breathtaking landscapes, rich history, and exciting activities of Fortune Island, located off the coast of Nasugbu, Batangas, Philippines. This 27-hectare island is renowned for its stunning cliffside views, ancient Greek-inspired structures, and pristine beaches, making it a unique destination for adventure seekers and history buffs.

Unveil the Beauty of Fortune Island

Fortune Island, situated off Nasugbu, Batangas, is a captivating destination known for its stunning cliffside views, ancient Greek-inspired structures, and pristine beaches. It was once a luxury resort but is now an abandoned yet fascinating spot for adventure seekers.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Acropolis: Features an Acropolis with Grecian pillars and statues, offering breathtaking views of the sea.
  • Marine Life: The surrounding waters are rich in marine biodiversity, perfect for snorkeling and diving.
  • Beach and Cliff Diving: Offers a pristine white sand beach and opportunities for cliff diving.
  • Historical Significance: The island is near the wreck of the San Diego, a Spanish galleon that sank in 1600.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Snorkeling and Diving: Explore the vibrant coral reefs and historical shipwrecks.
  • Island Hopping: Visit nearby islands for additional adventures.
  • Hiking: Hike to scenic viewpoints and explore the island's rugged landscapes.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of the Acropolis and the surrounding waters.

Fortune Island: Simple Island Dining

At Fortune Island, dining options are quite basic but still delicious, with a focus on Filipino comfort food. Enjoy grilled seafood, bbq skewers, and suman under the shade of beachside tents. The simple yet satisfying meals perfectly complement the island’s relaxing atmosphere, allowing you to enjoy the beautiful scenery while indulging in local flavors.

Why Visit Fortune Island?

  • Unique Landscapes: Offers stunning cliffside views and ancient Greek-inspired structures.
  • Adventure: Perfect for snorkeling, diving, and cliff diving.
  • Accessibility: Located near Manila, making it a convenient weekend getaway.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Island Access: Open daily; access via boat from Nasugbu.
  • Entrance Fees: Around ₱5,000 for a day trip, including boat transfers.
  • Guided Tours: Available for snorkeling and diving activities.

Fun Fact:

Fortune Island is famous for its Acropolis, which resembles the Parthenon, and its proximity to the wreck of the San Diego, a historical Spanish galleon. The island's unique blend of history and natural beauty makes it a must-visit destination for adventurers and history enthusiasts.
