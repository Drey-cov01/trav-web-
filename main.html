CALABARZON REPRESENTZ: ETIVAC

Cavite is a province in the CALABARZON region of Luzon, Philippines. It is known for hosting significant events during the Philippine Revolution against Spanish authority. The province is rich in cultural heritage, with numerous historical sites, including the Aguinaldo Shrine in Kawit, where Philippine independence was declared in 1898. Its proximity to Metro Manila makes it a popular destination for both historical tourists and residential purposes. Cavite's culture combines traditional Filipino values with a more contemporary global impact due to its developing infrastructure. The best time to visit Cavite is during the dry season, which runs from December to May, with cooler, more comfortable weather. The rainy season, from June to November, can bring heavy showers, limiting outdoor sightseeing. The primary language spoken in Cavite is Tagalog, which is also the official language of the Philippines, but Chabacano is also spoken. English is widely spoken in business and urban areas. The Philippine Peso (PHP) is the currency used in Cavite and throughout the Philippines, with most establishments accepting cash and ATMs in cities and major towns. Credit and debit cards are also accepted in developed towns. Mobile payment systems like GCash and PayMaya are also widely used.

Famous Tourist Attractions

The Aguinaldo Shrine is one of the most historically significant sites in Cavite. It was here that Emilio Aguinaldo, the first president of the Philippines, declared the country's independence from Spanish rule on June 12, 1898.

Aguinaldo Shrine (Kawit)

Highlights:

  • Flag-raising ceremony monument
  • Historical museum inside the shrine
  • Views of the surrounding landscape

Located in Tagaytay, the People's Park in the Sky offers breathtaking views of Taal Volcano and Taal Lake. Visitors can stroll through the park, enjoy the cool climate, and admire panoramic vistas.

People's Park in the Sky (Tagaytay)

Highlights:

  • Scenic views of Taal Volcano and the lake
  • Cool, fresh air from the higher elevation
  • The old guest house structure

Tucked away in Alfonso, Cavite, Balite Falls is a hidden gem, perfect for those looking for a peaceful nature retreat. The waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery, making it an ideal spot for relaxation.

Balite Falls (Alfonso)

Highlights:

  • A serene waterfall surrounded by nature
  • Refreshing natural pool for swimming
  • Easy access via a short trek from the entrance

The Maragondon River offers a serene escape with its calm waters and scenic surroundings. It’s perfect for nature trips like kayaking or simply enjoying a peaceful day by the water.

Maragondon River (Maragondon)

Highlights:

  • Kayaking and boat rides along the river
  • Peaceful and scenic surroundings for relaxing and picnicking
  • Lush greenery and natural beauty

Mt. Marami offers a challenging yet rewarding hike with a scenic view at the summit. The trek takes you through forest trails and rocky terrain, making it perfect for adventure seekers.

Mt. Marami (Maragondon)

Highlights:

  • Challenging and scenic hike
  • View of the surrounding mountains and towns
  • Great for hiking enthusiasts

Sky Ranch Tagaytay is a popular amusement park located in Tagaytay, known for its thrilling rides and stunning views of Taal Volcano and Taal Lake. It's a family-friendly destination that offers various attractions for all ages.

Sky Ranch (Tagaytay)

Highlights:

  • Sky Eye: One of the tallest Ferris wheels in the Philippines
  • Diverse rides suitable for all ages
  • Food stalls offering local and international cuisine

Taal Volcano, located in Talisay, Cavite, offers a breathtaking view of an active volcano surrounded by Taal Lake. It’s perfect for adventurers looking for a scenic boat ride and an exciting trek to the summit.

Taal Volcano (Talisay)

Highlights:

  • Boat ride to the island
  • Scenic views of Taal Lake and Volcano
  • Exciting trek to the summit

Corregidor Island is a historically rich destination with WWII-era military structures and memorials. Visitors can explore tunnels, bunkers, and enjoy panoramic views of Manila Bay.

Corregidor Island (Cavite)

Highlights:

  • Explore the Malinta Tunnel
  • Visit WWII-era memorials and structures
  • Stunning views of Manila Bay
