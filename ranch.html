Relax & Enjoy the Thrills of Sky Ranch

Discover the excitement, stunning views, and family-friendly atmosphere of Sky Ranch Tagaytay, located in Tagaytay City, Cavite, Philippines. This 5-hectare leisure park is renowned for its thrilling rides, panoramic views of Taal Lake and Volcano, and diverse dining options, making it a must-visit destination for families and adventure seekers.

Unveil the Thrills of Sky Ranch Tagaytay

Sky Ranch Tagaytay is a popular amusement park developed by SM Prime Holdings, Inc. It is known for its panoramic views of Taal Lake and Volcano, making it a perfect spot for both thrill-seekers and families.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Panoramic Views: Offers stunning views of Taal Lake and Volcano from various rides and viewpoints.
  • Thrilling Rides: Features a range of rides from gentle to adrenaline-pumping experiences.
  • Family-Friendly Environment: Suitable for all ages with diverse attractions and dining options.
  • Events Space: Hosts corporate events and social gatherings at The Venue.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Rides and Attractions: Enjoy thrilling rides like the Sky Eye and Super Viking.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of Taal Lake and Volcano.
  • Carnival Games: Engage in fun games and win prizes.
  • Horseback Riding: Explore the park on horseback.

Sky Ranch Tagaytay: Food with a View

Sky Ranch Tagaytay offers several restaurants and food stalls where you can enjoy Filipino favorites such as grilled meats, halo-halo, and empanada (savory pastry). The food here is casual, ideal for a light meal or snack while enjoying the views from the amusement park.

Why Visit Sky Ranch Tagaytay?

  • Thrilling Rides: Offers a mix of adrenaline-pumping and family-friendly attractions.
  • Panoramic Views: Provides stunning views of Taal Lake and Volcano.
  • Accessibility: Conveniently located in Tagaytay, making it a popular day trip destination from Manila.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Park Hours: Open from 10 AM to 10 PM on weekdays and 8 AM to 10 PM on weekends and holidays.
  • Entrance Fees: Vary depending on the package; ride-all-you-can tickets are available.
  • Promotions: Offers discounts and special promos, including birthday packages.

Fun Fact:

Sky Ranch Tagaytay is part of the SM Group's leisure projects, designed to provide a fun and memorable experience for visitors. The park's Sky Eye Ferris Wheel is one of the tallest in the country, offering breathtaking views of Taal Lake and Volcano.
