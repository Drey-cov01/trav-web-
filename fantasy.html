Relax & Explore the Mystique of Fantasy World

Discover the enchanting beauty and nostalgic charm of Fantasy World, an abandoned theme park located in Lemery, Batangas, Philippines. Originally envisioned as the "Disneyland of the Philippines," this Bavarian-inspired park has become a popular destination for photography, sightseeing, and quiet exploration.

Unveil the Beauty of Fantasy World

Fantasy World was originally built in 2001 to rival Disneyland but was left unfinished due to financial constraints. Today, it is maintained as a photo park and offers visitors a glimpse into its grand yet eerie charm.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Bavarian-Inspired Castle: The centerpiece of Fantasy World, perfect for photography and exploration.
  • Hanging Bridge: A relatively new addition offering panoramic views of Batangas’ landscape.
  • Throne Room: A classical-themed room where visitors can sit on a majestic throne for photos.
  • Scenic Views: Climb to the top of the main tower for breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and countryside.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of the castle, hanging bridge, and lush landscapes.
  • Exploration: Wander through the park’s grounds and discover its hidden gems.
  • Event Hosting: Use the park as a venue for photoshoots or intimate celebrations.

Fantasy World: A Fun Meal in a Fantasy Setting

While Fantasy World may not offer a wide variety of dining options, you can still find food stalls selling classic Filipino fast food. Hotdogs, french fries, and pancit (noodle dishes) are some of the quick bites available within the park. Some food vendors also offer halo-halo, the popular Filipino dessert made with crushed ice, sweet beans, and fruits.

Why Visit Fantasy World?

  • Unique Atmosphere: Offers a blend of grandeur and mystery with its abandoned yet well-preserved structures.
  • Photography Haven: Perfect for creative shoots with its medieval architecture and scenic views.
  • Accessibility: Located just a few hours from Metro Manila, making it an ideal day trip destination.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Operating Hours: Open daily from 8 AM to 5 PM.
  • Entrance Fees: ₱100 per person; professional photoshoot packages cost an additional ₱5,000.

Fun Fact:

Fantasy World has been featured in various Filipino TV shows and movies, including Wansapanataym and Majika. Despite its incomplete status, it continues to captivate visitors with its fairytale-like ambiance.
