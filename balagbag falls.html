Relax & Experience the Majestic Beauty of Balagbag Falls

Immerse yourself in the refreshing waters and serene ambiance of Balagbag Falls, nestled in the heart of Real, Quezon. This multi-tiered waterfall is a hidden gem known for its cascading waters, lush greenery, and tranquil atmosphere, perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Unveil the Beauty of Balagbag Falls

Balagbag Falls features crystal-clear waters that flow down from rocky cliffs into natural pools, creating a picturesque setting ideal for swimming and relaxation. Surrounded by dense forests, the falls offer a peaceful escape where visitors can enjoy nature's beauty, take scenic hikes, and unwind to the soothing sounds of cascading waters.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Two-Tiered Waterfall – Experience the beauty of cascading waters with both an upper and lower level.
  • Natural Pools – Enjoy a refreshing swim in the clear, cool pools at the base of the falls.
  • Scenic Surroundings – Be captivated by the lush forest backdrop and tranquil ambiance.
  • Accessible Adventure – A short hike from the road makes the falls an easy yet rewarding destination.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Swimming – Take a refreshing dip in the cool, natural pools.
  • Hiking – Enjoy a scenic trek through the lush forest trails leading to the falls.
  • Picnicking – Set up a relaxing picnic by the riverbanks with family and friends.
  • Nature Photography – Capture stunning shots of the cascading waterfalls and surrounding landscapes.

Food and Dining:

Balagbag Falls offers a simple dining experience with small local vendors nearby selling snacks, grilled food, and refreshments. Visitors are encouraged to bring their own food and drinks for a picnic-style meal by the falls. Enjoying a meal surrounded by nature’s beauty adds to the relaxing and refreshing experience.

Why Visit "balagbag falls"

  • Hidden Gem – Discover one of Quezon’s less crowded yet stunning natural attractions.
  • Family-Friendly Destination – Perfect for a fun day trip with loved ones.
  • Instagram-Worthy Views – Capture beautiful shots of the cascading falls and lush surroundings.
  • Eco-Tourism Spot – Support local communities while enjoying the area's pristine environment.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Open daily from 6:00 AM – 5:00 PM
  • Fee: ₱20 – ₱30 per person
  • Cottage Rentals: ₱300 – ₱500 for day use
  • Parking Fee: ₱50 – ₱100 (depending on vehicle type)

Fun Fact:

Balagbag Falls is known as the “Twin Falls” because of its two cascading tiers, making it one of the most photogenic waterfalls in Real, Quezon. The name “Balagbag” translates to “tilted” or “slanted”, describing the unique angle of the waterfall’s rock formations!
