Relax & Explore the Artistic Beauty of Pinto Art Museum

Discover the vibrant world of contemporary art, stunning architecture, and lush gardens of Pinto Art Museum, located in Antipolo, Rizal, Philippines. This unique museum is renowned for its eclectic design, blending art, nature, and culture, making it a must-visit destination for art enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

Unveil the Beauty of Pinto Art Museum

Pinto Art Museum is a contemporary art museum complex located in Antipolo, Rizal. It was founded by Dr. Joven Cuanang in 2010 as a gateway for modern and contemporary art, showcasing over 300 artworks by local artists.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Eclectic Architecture: The museum features six buildings designed by Antonio Leaño in an organic and eclectic style, blending seamlessly with nature.
  • Art Collections: Houses a diverse collection of modern paintings, sculptures, and art installationsSilangan Gardens: Situated within a two-hectare botanical garden, offering a serene environment for art appreciation.
  • Rotating Exhibitions: Regularly hosts new exhibitions showcasing contemporary Filipino art.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Photography: Capture stunning shots of the museum's unique architecture and art pieces.
  • Exploration: Wander through the gardens and explore the museum's various galleries.
  • Art Workshops: Participate in art classes and workshops.
  • Literary Workshops: Engage in writing sessions and literary discussions.

Pinto Art Museum: Art and Culinary Delights

At Pinto Art Museum, the dining experience matches the creative atmosphere. The museum features a café where visitors can enjoy a selection of local delicacies and refreshments. The café is surrounded by lush gardens, making it the perfect spot to relax and enjoy a peaceful meal after exploring the art galleries. Try Filipino favorites like pancit or suman, paired with freshly brewed coffee or refreshing fruit juices.

Why Visit Pinto Art Museum?

  • Unique Architecture: Offers a blend of art and nature with its open-air design.
  • Artistic Experience: Provides a platform for contemporary Filipino art.
  • Accessibility: Located near Manila, making it a convenient day trip destination.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Museum Hours: Open daily; specific hours may vary.
  • Entrance Fees: Around ₱250 per person, with discounts for students, seniors, and PWDs.
  • Guided Tours: Available upon request.

Fun Fact:

Pinto Art Museum is considered one of the most Instagrammable museums globally, alongside the Louvre and the Met, due to its unique architecture and scenic surroundings.
