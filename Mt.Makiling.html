"Unveil the Mystique of Mt. Makiling"

Mt. Makiling is a dormant stratovolcano located in Laguna and Batangas, Philippines. It is famous for its lush forest reserve, rich biodiversity, and cultural significance. The mountain is also shrouded in local folklore, believed to be home to Maria Makiling, a guardian spirit who protects the mountain and its surroundings.
