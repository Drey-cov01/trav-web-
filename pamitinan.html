Relax & Explore the Adventure of Mt. Pamitinan

Discover the scenic landscapes, rich history, and exciting activities of Mt. Pamitinan, located in Sitio Wawa, Barangay San Rafael, Rodriguez, Rizal, Philippines. This beginner-friendly mountain is part of the Pamitinan Protected Landscape and offers stunning views of the Sierra Madre mountain range and the Wawa River.

Unveil the Beauty of Mt. Pamitinan

Mt. Pamitinan is a 426-meter high mountain located in the Sierra Madre mountain range. It is a popular hiking destination for beginners due to its accessible trails and scenic views.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Scenic Views: Offers panoramic views of the Sierra Madre mountain range and the Wawa River.
  • Pamitinan Cave: A historical site used by Katipuneros during the Philippine Revolution, featuring the inscription "Viva la Independencia Filipina".
  • Wawa Dam: A nearby recreational spot for swimming and picnicking.
  • Limestone Formations: Features unique limestone cliffs and rock formations.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Hiking and Rock Climbing: Engage in hiking and rock climbing activities with breathtaking views.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of the mountains and surrounding landscapes.
  • Nature Walks: Explore the trails and learn about the area's biodiversity.
  • Event Hosting: Use the scenic areas for intimate events or celebrations.

Mt. Paminitan: Simple Yet Satisfying Meals

After a rigorous climb up Mt. Paminitan, the local eateries near the mountain serve hearty and satisfying meals. Local restaurants offer popular Filipino comfort food such as batchoy, tapsilog (fried rice, egg, and marinated beef), and suman (sweet sticky rice). These meals are simple but filling, perfect for recharging after a tough hike.

Why Visit Mt. Pamitinan?

  • Natural Beauty: Offers a serene and natural atmosphere, perfect for relaxation and exploration.
  • Historical Significance: Features the Pamitinan Cave, a site of historical importance during the Philippine Revolution.
  • Accessibility: Located near Metro Manila, making it a convenient day trip destination.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Mountain Access: Open daily; specific hours may vary.
  • Entrance Fees: Minimal fees apply for hiking and cave exploration.
  • Guided Tours: Available upon request.
