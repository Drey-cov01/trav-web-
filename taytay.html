"Escape to the Serenity of Taytay Falls"

Experience the untouched beauty of nature at Taytay Falls, a hidden paradise in Majayjay, Laguna. Known for its icy-cold spring water, this breathtaking waterfall is the perfect getaway for adventure seekers and those looking to relax in a peaceful, nature-filled environment.

"A Natural Wonder in Laguna"

Taytay Falls is a two-tiered waterfall with a steady stream of water flowing from the Mount Banahaw watershed. Its pristine natural pool offers a tranquil and cooling retreat, making it one of the most visited waterfalls in Laguna.
