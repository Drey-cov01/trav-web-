Relax & Explore the Natural Beauty of Masungi Georeserve

Discover the stunning landscapes, rich biodiversity, and exciting activities of Masungi Georeserve, located in the southern Sierra Madre range in Baras, Rizal, Philippines. This conservation area is renowned for its rugged limestone karst peaks, lush montane rainforests, and unique geological formations, making it a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Unveil the Beauty of Masungi Georeserve

Masungi Georeserve is a protected area located approximately 51 kilometers from Manila, known for its unique limestone formations and diverse biodiversity. It is home to over 400 species of flora and fauna, including rare and endangered species like the Philippine eagle.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Limestone Karst Peaks: Offers stunning views of rugged geological formations and surrounding rainforests.
  • Caves and Rock Formations: Explore caves like Yungib ni Ruben and marvel at the intricate rock formations.
  • Suspension Bridges: Cross the Sapot for a panoramic view of the Sierra Madre and Laguna de Bay.
  • Biodiversity: Home to diverse flora and fauna, including unique plants like the Purple Jade Vine.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Hiking and Exploration: Wander through the trails and explore the georeserve's unique landscapes.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of the geological formations and wildlife.
  • Nature Walks: Engage in guided nature walks to learn about the area's biodiversity.
  • Event Hosting: Use the georeserve as a venue for intimate celebrations.

Masungi Georeserve: A Refreshing Meal in Nature

After an adventurous day of hiking and exploring the Masungi Georeserve, visitors can unwind and savor light meals at the reserve’s café. Enjoy freshly prepared sandwiches, fruit juices, and local snacks while taking in the beautiful surroundings. The food offerings are light yet satisfying, perfect for refueling after your outdoor adventures.

Why Visit Masungi Georeserve?

  • Natural Beauty: Offers a serene and natural atmosphere, perfect for relaxation and exploration.
  • Conservation Efforts: Participate in forest restoration and learn about conservation initiatives
  • Accessibility: Located near Manila, making it a convenient day trip destination.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Georeserve Hours: Open daily; specific hours may vary.
  • Entrance Fees: Around ₱1,500 per person for guided tours.
  • Guided Tours: Available upon request.

Fun Fact:

Masungi Georeserve is a model for balanced man-nature interactions and has received numerous awards for its conservation efforts, including recognition from the World Conservation Congress and the United Nations.
