Relax & Explore the Spiritual Beauty of Monte Maria Shrine

Discover the serene ambiance, rich history, and breathtaking views of Montemaria Shrine, located in Barangay Pagkilatan, Batangas City, Philippines. This pilgrimage site is renowned for its stunning architecture and the world's tallest statue of the Virgin Mary, known as the Mother of All Asia – Tower of Peace.

Unveil the Beauty of Monte Maria Shrine

Montemaria Shrine, officially known as the Montemaria International Pilgrimage & Conference Center, is a prominent spiritual destination in Batangas City. It features the iconic Mother of All Asia – Tower of Peace, the world's tallest statue of the Virgin Mary, standing at 98.15 meters.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Mother of All Asia Statue: The centerpiece of Montemaria, symbolizing peace and unity for all of Asia.
  • Panoramic Views: Offers stunning views of Batangas Bay and the surrounding landscape.
  • Miracle Glass Walk: A unique spiritual activity where visitors walk on glass.
  • Conference and Retreat Facilities: Ideal for spiritual retreats and conferences.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Prayer and Meditation: Engage in prayer and meditation sessions in a serene atmosphere.
  • Exploration: Wander through the shrine grounds and explore its scenic views.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of the statue and the surrounding landscape.
  • Event Hosting: Use the shrine as a venue for intimate celebrations or events.

Monte Maria Shrine: A Peaceful Meal in a Sacred Place

At Monte Maria Shrine, small food stalls and nearby restaurants provide a peaceful setting to enjoy traditional Filipino meals like sinigang na baboy (pork in sour broth) and adobong manok (chicken adobo). The dining spots here offer a calm and quiet atmosphere, perfect for reflection and relaxation after visiting the shrine.

Why Visit Monte Maria Shrine?

  • Spiritual Experience: Offers a peaceful and spiritual environment for reflection and prayer.
  • Architectural Beauty: Features stunning architecture with the iconic Mother of All Asia statue.
  • Accessibility: Located near Batangas City, making it a convenient day trip or weekend getaway.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Shrine Hours: Open daily; specific hours may vary.
  • Entrance Fees: Generally free, though donations are appreciated.
  • Guided Tours: Available upon request.

Fun Fact:

Monte Maria Shrine is strategically located overlooking Batangas Bay, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding waters. The shrine is a testament to faith and unity, attracting pilgrims and tourists alike with its majestic statue and serene ambiance.
