Relax & Enjoy the Natural Beauty of Balite Falls

Discover the serene ambiance, stunning landscapes, and exciting activities of Balite Falls, located in Amadeo, Cavite, Philippines. This picturesque waterfall is a hidden gem in the province, known for its natural beauty and accessibility from Manila.

Unveil the Beauty of Balite Falls

Balite Falls is situated in the historical province of Cavite, known for its natural attractions and cultural heritage. The falls are part of a resort, making it easily accessible via a paved road from the main highway.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Waterfall: Features a main waterfall with a swimming area and a smaller, more serene waterfall upstream.
  • Natural Setting: Offers lush green landscapes and a meandering stream.
  • Accessibility: Located near Manila, making it a convenient day trip destination.
  • Cultural Significance: Part of Amadeo's natural wonders, contributing to the local economy through tourism.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Nature Walks: Explore the surrounding trails and enjoy the natural scenery.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of the waterfall and its surroundings.
  • Swimming: Enjoy swimming in the clear waters of the main falls.
  • Picnicking: Relax with family and friends in designated picnic areas.

Balite Falls: Dining with Nature

After enjoying the serenity of Balite Falls, nearby food stalls offer simple Filipino dishes like sinigang, grilled pork, and rice cakes. The ambiance is relaxed and rustic, allowing you to enjoy your meal while immersed in the surrounding natural beauty.

Why Visit Balite Falls?

  • Natural Beauty: Offers a serene and natural atmosphere, perfect for relaxation.
  • Accessibility: Located near Manila, making it a convenient day trip destination.
  • Cultural Significance: Contributes to the local economy and cultural heritage of Amadeo.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Resort Hours: Open daily; specific hours may vary.
  • Entrance Fees: ₱75 per person.
  • Cottage Rentals: Available for rent, priced from ₱200 to ₱500.

Fun Fact:

Balite Falls is one of Cavite's natural wonders, attracting tourists and supporting local businesses. The area has seen significant economic growth due to nature-based tourism, providing stable income for local residents.
