Relax & Enjoy the Natural Beauty of Daranak Falls

Discover the serene ambiance, stunning landscapes, and exciting activities of Daranak Falls, located in the municipality of Tanay, Rizal, Philippines. This picturesque waterfall is a popular tourist destination, known for its natural beauty and tranquil atmosphere.

Unveil the Beauty of Daranak Falls

Daranak Falls is a 14-meter high waterfall nestled in the Sierra Madre mountains. It is renowned for its jade green waters and serene surroundings, making it a perfect escape for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Waterfall: Offers a stunning view of cascading water into a 30 feet deep catch basin.
  • Natural Pool: Enjoy swimming in the cool waters at the base of the falls.
  • ●Picnic Grounds: Relax with family and friends in designated picnic areas.
  • ●Surrounding Trails: Explore the lush forests and trails around the falls.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Swimming: Dive into the natural pool for a refreshing swim.
  • Hiking: Explore the nearby trails and scenic viewpoints.
  • Photography: Capture stunning views of the waterfall and its surroundings.
  • Event Hosting: Use the falls as a backdrop for intimate celebrations.

Daranak Falls: Picnic by the Falls

Although Daranak Falls is more about nature and adventure, there are several picnic spots around the falls where you can enjoy a simple meal with friends and family. Local vendors also sell Filipino snacks such as banana cue, coconut juice, and kakanin (rice cakes), providing a true taste of local life. Bring your own food to enjoy a relaxed, outdoor meal by the falls.

Why Visit Daranak Falls?

  • Natural Beauty: Offers a serene and natural atmosphere, perfect for relaxation.
  • Adventure: Suitable for swimming, hiking, and nature exploration.
  • Accessibility: Located near Manila, making it a convenient day trip destination.

Operating Hours & Fees:

  • Falls Access: Open daily until 5 PM; no overnight swimming allowed.
  • Around ₱50-75 per person.
  • Cottage Rentals: Available for rent, with prices starting at ₱300.

Fun Fact:

Daranak Falls is named from the Tagalog word "Dadanak", meaning "to flow", reflecting the continuous flow of water from the falls. It is a popular spot for family outings and nature enthusiasts due to its picturesque landscapes and accessible location.
