Relax & Experience the Cultural Heritage of Villa Escudero Plantations and Resort

Step into a world of rich history, natural beauty, and Filipino traditions at Villa Escudero Plantations and Resort, located in Tiaong, Quezon. This premier destination offers a unique blend of cultural heritage, scenic landscapes, and warm hospitality, making it an unforgettable retreat for families and travelers.

Unveil the Beauty of Villa Escudero Plantations and Resort

Villa Escudero features lush coconut plantations, a private museum showcasing Filipino artifacts, and the famous Labasin Waterfalls Restaurant, where guests can enjoy traditional Filipino cuisine while dining at the foot of a man-made waterfall. With its serene surroundings, cultural performances, and bamboo rafting experiences, the resort provides a perfect balance of relaxation and cultural immersion.

Key Features and Attractions:

  • Waterfall Restaurant – Dine by a man-made waterfall with water flowing around your feet.
  • Cultural Showcase – Enjoy traditional Filipino music, dance, and a heritage museum.
  • Nature Experience – Explore coconut plantations, bamboo rafting, and carabao rides.
  • Scenic Retreat – Relax in lush greenery and tranquil surroundings.

Activities and Entertainment:

  • Waterfalls Restaurant (Labasin Waterfalls Restaurant) – Dine at a unique restaurant where tables are set in a shallow stream at the foot of a man-made waterfall.
  • Cultural Show & Museum – Enjoy traditional Filipino cultural performances and visit the Escudero Private Museum, showcasing historical artifacts and religious relics.
  • Carabao Cart Rides – Experience a fun, scenic ride around the plantation aboard a traditional carabao-drawn cart, accompanied by live music.
  • Bamboo Rafting – Paddle along the Labasin Lake on a native balsa (bamboo raft) for a relaxing nature experience.

A Taste of Paradise in Every Bite

Delight in the taste of authentic Filipino cuisine, served in a picturesque setting surrounded by nature. Enjoy locally-sourced ingredients and time-honored recipes that offer a true taste of the Philippines.

Why Visit "Villa Escudero Plantations"

  • Unique Dining Experience: Enjoy a one-of-a-kind meal at the Labasin Waterfalls Restaurant, where you can dine at the foot of a waterfall with refreshing water flowing around your feet.
  • rRich Cultural Heritage: Discover Filipino traditions through cultural dance performances, live music, and a museum showcasing historical artifacts and antiques.
  • Nature and Adventure: Experience bamboo rafting, carabao rides, and scenic walks through coconut plantations, perfect for nature lovers and adventurers.
  • Relaxing Getaway: Escape the busy city life and unwind in the resort's serene surroundings, with lush landscapes and tranquil rivers.

Fun Fact:

The Labasin Waterfalls Restaurant at Villa Escudero is one of the few waterfall restaurants in the world! Guests can enjoy their meals with their feet submerged in cool, flowing spring water — making it not just a dining experience, but a relaxing and refreshing one-of-a-kind adventure.
